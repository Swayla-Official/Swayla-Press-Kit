About Swayla

Swayla is a soulful voice emerging from the crossroads of R&B / Soul, Afro-fusion. Rooted in soulfully pop rhythms, her music bridges a traditional RnB flow with her own alternative, effortless cadence. Accompanied by her band “The Black Notes”, their synchronicity is palpable and electric. Drawing inspiration from her British, African, and Portuguese heritage, Swayla’s sound is rich, raw, and deeply emotive.

Whether performing intimate ballads or rhythmic anthems, Swayla invites listeners into a world where vulnerability and strength coexist in every note. Beyond her artistry, she is a passionate advocate for cultural preservation and education, hosting her radio show Rhythm & Bloom on Dejavu FM, uplifting artists and communities alike.
