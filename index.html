About Swayla

Hi, I'm Swayla. Welcome to my world. I'm a soulful person, so everything I do, "it's coming from the heart"... That's the reason why i've decided to type this out myself. In a world of automation, I wanna show you guys parts of my authentic self. I made this website as an invitation for you guys to see through my lens, my emotions. Let's start at the root. Growing up in a PALOP HOME - "African Countries with Portuguese Official Language", hearing: Creolo, Portuguese, English the sounds i would hear, the deep, soulful Sax on Rui Sangara's "Maskote D'oro"; the full bands, Electric Guitars, and vocal diversity and range in songs like: Pacensera 'Justino Delgado', would touch me deeply in every crevice of my being, right to my knees. Music that would reach the depth's of me. The ability to feel so intensely has always been a part of me, but these songs from home unlocked something that craved to be expressed. Cesaria Evora's tone and timber in songs like 'Sodade' has always transported me to places i've never been. I felt the struggle, the sadness, the loss, the loneliness. I realised that i too felt these things, i carried them for myself, and the ones that came before me. It really is that deep. My estate growing up, had so much culture. Neighbours of every culture, we came together. The Carribbean culture is a huge part of my development. Childhood friends, neighbours, family. They all poured into me. My sound is rooted in Rhythm and Blues. But i'm a melting pot at the end of the day... Which makes me experimental...

I curated the Rhythm & Bloom show on DejaVu FM. I needed a space to speak and be spoken to. I needed a place to showcase the art and creatives around me, the talent i'm thankful enough to be surrounded by everyday. All around me are inspirational people, building beautiful lives and this is a chance for me to spotlight them and their work. Music is healing, Music saves, Music evolves minds and hearts. It has to be well intentioned though, thats the caveat...
