About Swayla

Swayla is a soulful voice rooted in R&B, Afro-fusion and alternative soul. Her work bridges vulnerability and strength, tradition and experimentation.

She is also a radio host and cultural curator, spotlighting emerging voices through Rhythm & Bloom on DejaVu FM.
